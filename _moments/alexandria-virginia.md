## Alexandria, Virginia

Old Town Alexandria is one of my favorite places for quiet walks and reflective afternoons.
The riverside paths and historic streets made this trip both calm and energizing.

### Highlights

- Sunset walk along the waterfront
- Neighborhood coffee shops and bookstores
- Short nature trip outside the city
