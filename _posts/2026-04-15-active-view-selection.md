## Overview

Active View Selection with Perturbed Gaussian Ensemble explores how to select informative views for tomographic reconstruction with improved robustness and performance.

## Highlights

- Perturbed Gaussian Ensemble based strategy for view selection
- Improved reconstruction quality with efficient sampling
- Open resources available for reproducible research
